The Paycheck Restoration Act: Interactive Model

Adjust the assumptions to see how the proposal performs under different scenarios.

This tool lets you explore the key design choices in the Paycheck Restoration Act, a proposed federal consumption-based tax that replaces income, payroll, and corporate income taxes. The most important variable is the revenue target. Most analyses assume current revenue ($5.20T), which leaves the same structural deficit current law produces. The balanced budget option ($7.20T) is what we actually want. All numbers are based on publicly available data from BLS, BEA, IRS, and CBO. This is a model, not a guarantee. See the README for the full proposal.
